OUR ROOTS

MISSYKIT Brewing Company was created in 2020 by a handful of life-long friends with a serious passion for beer. We scraped together enough money to purchase a derelict warehouse unit in our hometown of Brighton, UK and began to brew. Many hours, hops and hangovers later we were finally proud enough to release our first beer - MISSYKIT House Pale. And the rest is history.

OUR ETHOS

WE BELIEVE there are many essential ingredients which make a beer taste great. Kindness, equality and sustainability are just three and go a long way to giving every one of our loyal beer drinkers that perfect "ahhh" moment when they take that first sip. Fresh, quality ingredients, care, love and passion have been a staple at MISSYKIT since we started, and that will never change.

OUR FUTURE

TOMORROW BRINGS another opportunity for us to create something new. Our mission is to never get bored of doing what we love, and in turn to make sure YOU never get bored of what we make. We promise to keep doing what we do in the best way we can, and hope you are right beside us on this incredible journey we've forged together. Stay tuned for news on our new brews, collabs and events coming in 2021.
